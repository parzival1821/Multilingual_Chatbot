## Test Tasks

Each participant should be asked to complete the following tasks:

1. Choose a language.
2. Fill the eligibility profile.
3. Click `Find Schemes`.
4. Read the top recommendation.
5. Explain in their own words why the scheme was suggested.
6. Click `View documents`.
7. Identify at least two documents required.
8. Ask one follow-up question.
9. Rate ease of use from 1 to 5.

## Comprehension Questions

Ask after showing a recommendation:

1. Which scheme was recommended first?
2. Why was it recommended?
3. What is one benefit of the scheme?
4. What is one document needed?
5. Where should final eligibility be verified?

Score each user out of 5.
